Redesigning the Ky Michaels Salon Home Page

Webpage:

https://kymichaelssalon.com/

Ky Michaels Salon is a small business on Wickenden Street in Providence. Their home page is the first page you see when you click on their website. It lists the salonâ€™s hours, address, and other important information about the salon.

Ky Michaels Salon is the salon I go to in Providence to cut my hair, but I usually call in because their website is hard to navigate and there is no link to book an appointment. I chose to redesign the home page to help make the website interaction easier.

Objective:

The objective of the responsive redesign project is to improve the usability of the Ky Michaels Salon home page through responsive web design techniques.

Usability Issues:

  • Embedded YouTube video takes up most of the page, making it inefficient.
  • Title on the browser tab is not the salon name, making navigation harder.
  • Booking an appointment and pricing information are hard to find.
  • Inconsistent drop-down menus in the header.
  • Lack of a "Shop" option and unclear product categorization.
  • Home page only includes basic information, which can be misleading.

Accessibility Issues:

  • Webpage is not well-adapted for screen readers.
  • Missing alternative text for images.
  • Low contrast icons in the footer.
